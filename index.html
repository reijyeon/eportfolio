8 Online Study Habits

1. Create to-do lists

To-do lists are extremely helpful to remind us what task we have not completed yet. Organizing our tasks with a list can make everything much more manageable and make us feel grounded.

2. Commit to your schedule

Since we are using our electronic devices for online schooling, there too many distractions that may slowdown progress, which is why it is essential to commit to your schedule.

3. Take a break

Studying for long periods of time wihout a break can be exhausting, taking a break is essential to help refresh our mind and body in order to reduce stress.

4. Use the Internet for additional information

Sometimes there are topics we could not fully grasp, which is why looking for other resources or study materials in the internet could be really helpful to deepen our understanding or knowledge about a subject.

5. Check the E-Learning apps regularly

Staying updated is critical in online classes, so we should regularly check our devices to not miss any requirements or deadlines.

6. Stay Hydrated

Becoming thirsty can be a major distraction for us students, which is why staying hydrated could help us to stay focused at our tasks.

7. Take care of our devices

Our devices is our primary tool to access our subject’s modules, which is we occasionally check to ensure that they are running optimally.

8. Sleep Properly

Being sleep-deprived makes it really difficult to focus or pay attention, which is why we should have proper sleep routine.

3 Short Term Goals

1. Create a Study Plans Weekly

Planning ahead is crucial to starting your week off right, having an organized weekly study plan help us learn more efficiently at a faster rate because our tasks could be simpler if we can manage our time properly.

2. Make Projects Manageable

It’s very easy to become overwhelmed when you’re given a large project or assignment with a looming deadline, but by breaking it down into small sections and giving yourself a deadline for each one, the project becomes more achievable.

3. Schedule Time for Passions

It’s important to schedule time not only for commitments related to school or other activities but also for time you are spending with friends or loved ones. Create time for your hobbies and passions because without this, it’s easy to burn out.

3 Long Term Goals

1. Finish the Semester

This is the primary goal for us students every semester, is to complete the requirements needed to pass the semester, It would be really rewarding to know that all your hard work paid off in the end.

2. Be equipped with Skills required in the Real World

Learning and practicing the skills needed for your Career Path is crucial for us students, we should allot time and effort to master our crafts in order to have your dream job in the future.

3. Develop a great Work Ethic

Having a good work ethic is a necessity, you should be able to have the self-discipline, patience, and determination among others in order to push through against difficult obstacles you will encounter.
